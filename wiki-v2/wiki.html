Flickering

During a hunt, ghosts will flicker in and out of existance, alternating between visible and invisible multiple times every few seconds. There are a couple of ghosts that can be identified by their flickering during a hunt: (1) an Oni will be more visible during a hunt and (2) the Phantom will be invisible for longer periods of time.
