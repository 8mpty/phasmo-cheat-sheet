Flickering

During a hunt, ghosts will flicker in and out of existance, alternating between visible and invisible multiple times every few seconds. There are a couple of ghosts that can be identified by their flickering during a hunt: (1) an Oni will be more visible during a hunt and (2) the Phantom will be invisible for longer periods of time.

Wishes

"I wish to see the ghost"
  • Pro: Ghost appears at its current location
  • Con: After the event, a hunt will start and player vision is darkened for the duration of the event and hunt
"I wish for activity"
  • Pro: Doubles all ghost activity for 2 min
  • Con: Breaker set to broken and front door locked for 2 min
"I wish for sanity"
  • Pro: Averages everyone's sanity to 50%
  • Con: Sets ghost's favorite room to a random room and increases current sanity drain by 50%
"I wish the ghost was trapped"
  • Pro: Ghost locked in its room for 1 min where it cannot hunt, roam, or do abilities
  • Con: Locks doors in the players room and hunt starts after the 1 min period (25% doors will unlock after grace period)
"I wish for life"
  • Pro: Revive a dead player
  • Con: 50% chance that the wishing player will die
"I wish to be safe"
  • Pro: Unblocks the nearest blocked hiding spot
  • Con: Breaks the light in that room and ghost is able to sense or hear the player from any distance
"I wish to leave"
  • Pro: Unlocks all exit doors (works during hunts)
  • Con: Player slowness and darkened vision for 5 secs
"I wish for knowledge"
  • Pro: Removes 1 incorrect evidence from the journal and crosses out associated ghosts
  • Con: Starts a hunt and player vision and audio is reduced the rest of that round
"I wish for [weather]"
  • Pro: Changes the weather to the one specified
  • Con: Drops sanity by 25%
"I wish for anything"
  • Randomly chooses one of the above wishes

Questions

"Where are you?"
  • Sanity Cost: 50%
  • Gives the current room of the ghost (not the favorite room)
Are you here? / Are you close?"
  • Sanity Cost: 20%
  • Tells if the ghost is in the same room as the player
"Where is the bone?"
  • Sanity Cost: 50%
  • Gives the room of the bone
"Hide and seek?"
  • Sanity Cost: 0% (breaks board)
  • Counts down from 5 and starts a hunt
"Do you respond to everyone?"
  • Sanity Cost: 20%
  • Replies with if the ghosts wants to be alone when using the spirit box
"How old are you?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns the current age of the ghost. If the number changes, the ghost is a Thaye
"When did you die?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a random number
"What is my sanity?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • 'Healthy' (Sanity > 80%), 'Good' (60-80%), 'Average' (40-60%), 'Bad' (20-40%), 'Awful' (<20%)
"How insane am I?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • 'Not Very' (Sanity > 50%), 'Very' (25-50%), 'Insane' (<25%)
"Am I insane?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • 'No' (Sanity > 90%), 'Maybe' (20-90%), 'Yes' (<20%)
"How did you die?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns the ghost's cause of death
"How do you feel?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a randomly selected emotion
"Why are you here?"
  • Sanity Cost: 5%
  • Returns a randomly selected reason

Tarot Cards

Tarot Cards

Tarot cards can be picked up, and you can draw one card at a time.
Each Tarot card deck contains 10 randomly generated cards.

The Tower

Causes an interaction and doubles all ghost activity for 20 seconds.

  • 20% Draw Chance

The Wheel of Fortune

The player who draws the card will experience either a gain or loss of sanity, depending on the burn color of the card.

Green burn: 25% Sanity gain
Red burn: 25% Sanity loss

  • 20% Draw Chance

The Fool

No effect is applied when drawing this card.

Randomly selects another card to appear as, before turning into The Fool as it burns.

  • 17% Draw Chance
  • (100% during hunts)

The Devil

Triggers a Ghost Event towards the nearest player to the ghost.

  • 10% Draw Chance

Death

Triggers a cursed hunt.

  • 10% Draw Chance

The Hermit

Teleports the ghost back to its ghost room and traps it there for 1 minute.

This does not affect hunts and ghost events.

  • 10% Draw Chance

The Sun

The player who draws the card will have their sanity fully restored to 100%

  • 5% Draw Chance

The Moon

The player who draws the card will have their sanity instantly set to 0%.

  • 5% Draw Chance

The High Priestess

Revives a randomly chosen dead player at their corpse.

If no one is currently dead, it will revive the next player who dies. Multiple cards do not stack.
(A player cannot die twice in the same hunt)

  • 2% Draw Chance

The Hanged Man

Instantly kills the player.

If 'Friendly Ghost' custom difficulty option is turned on, this will always turn into "The Fool".

  • 1% Draw Chance
